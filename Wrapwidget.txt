//"Wrap with widget" In Flutter Android Studio//

Steps:

step 1: go to your Android Studio settings 
step 2: search "assistant" in searchbox
step 3: then you will see on left side Editor -> Intentions
step 4: double click on intentions 
step 5: then you will see Dart, in that Enable "Quick assist powered by the Dart Analysis Server"
step 6: Save and closed, retart Android Studio It's Work!!! <:
